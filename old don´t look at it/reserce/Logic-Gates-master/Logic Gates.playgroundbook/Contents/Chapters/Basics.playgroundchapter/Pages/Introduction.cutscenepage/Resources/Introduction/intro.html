Logic gates are standardized physical devices which can be designed to implement Boolean algebra. Logic circuits can easily be assembled with logic gates. Then we have multiplexers, registers, arithmetic logic units (ALUs), and computer memory, all the way up through complete microprocessors, which may contain more than 100 million gates. And finally, mobile phones, tablets, and laptops. This playground will introduce you to logic gates, which are the very foundation of our digital life.
